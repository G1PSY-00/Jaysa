Astronomy & Exploration

Astronomy is the science of studying space, stars, planets, and the universe, while space exploration is the physical journey (human or robotic) into space, both driven by curiosity about our cosmic home, leading to profound scientific discoveries (origins of life, universe's fate) and tangible benefits like GPS, satellite tech, and better Earth understanding. These fields intertwine: astronomy provides the knowledge, and exploration provides the tools (telescopes, probes) and unique perspectives (from space) to expand that knowledge, answering big questions about our place in the cosmos and fostering innovation.
