Solar System

The Solar System is our cosmic neighborhood, centered around the Sun, a star, and includes everything gravitationally bound to it: eight planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune) with their moons, dwarf planets (like Pluto), asteroids, comets, and dust, all orbiting in elliptical paths within the Milky Way galaxy. It formed from a collapsing cloud of dust and gas about 4.6 billion years ago, with the Sun igniting at the center and planets forming from the remaining disk.
